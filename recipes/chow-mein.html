Description

Chow mein is a popular stir-fried noodle dish made with vegetables, soy sauce, and optional proteins like chicken or shrimp. It's quick, flavorful, and packed with texture.

Ingredients

  • Noodles (chow mein or egg noodles)
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Bell pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Chili sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles until just tender, then drain and toss with a little oil.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or pan and saut√© garlic and onions.
  3. Add sliced vegetables and stir-fry on high heat.
  4. Mix in the noodles along with soy sauce, vinegar, and chili sauce.
  5. Toss well until everything is combined and heated through. Garnish and serve.
  6. ENJOY!
