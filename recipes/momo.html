Momo Recipe

Home

Description

Momos are steamed dumplings filled with spiced vegetables or meat, popular in Tibetan and Nepali cuisine. Soft, juicy, and served with a spicy dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Water
  • Cabbage (finely chopped)
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Green chili (optional)
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Optional: minced chicken or paneer for non-veg/veg filling

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, salt, and water; knead until smooth and let it rest.
  2. Make the filling by sautéing chopped veggies (or meat) with garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and seasoning.
  3. Roll the dough into small discs and add a spoonful of filling to each.
  4. Fold and seal the momos into your desired shape (half-moon or pleated).
  5. Steam the momos for 10–12 minutes until cooked and translucent.
  6. ENJOY!
